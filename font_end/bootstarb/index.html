Knowing Kite Classroom

Kite Classroom is a web based academic platform to perform different educational activities for students, teachers and parents. Kite Classroom provides the opportunity to learn and to interact and ensure accountability for teachers, students and parents. The exceptionality of this web based portal is; it has been designed to accelerate the ICTE based skills of teachers, parents and students by providing educational means such as contents, evaluation and assessment related tools, interaction based platform, etc.. They will use this portal to upgrade their learnings through practicing or seeking education related information by searching different smart contents that has been already included in this web portal and throwing questions to educators.

The focusing part of this portal is for the first time in Bangladesh it has been started working with the domain (Curriculum Prescribed) of secondary grades. So that it is possible for educators to track the domain based competencies of learners and particular learner groups. As a result they will be able to put significant intervention or remedial actions to develop the efficiency in learners' learning fragile part. » More
